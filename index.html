College History

According to Hindu philosophy, out of 84 lakh yonis in this universe, Human (manav) is considered the best among all. For the maintenance of a healthy human life, Lord Brahma gifted Ayurveda before creating the Universe.

In 1921, the Brahma Charya Ashram Trust was established by Param Pujya Swami Dwaraka Duttaji Maharaj in Indore‚ÄîThe Nagri of Maa Ahilya. Initially, Ayurveda courses were run by this trust.

In 1964, this course got affiliated with Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya Indore (M.P.), and the degree course was started by the university. By the sincere efforts of the staff and state leaders, the Madhya Pradesh Government took over this institution in 1972, renaming it GOVT. ASHTANG AYURVED COLLEGE, Indore.
