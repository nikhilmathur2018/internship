Department of Rachana Sharir

Introduction

One who has though knowledge of body (Sharir Rachana) is a good physician and he/she can give pleasure to whole world. This is the basis of knowing the Sharir Rachana and we deal in this. Sharir Rachana is the basis of the entire subject as by this subject only student come to know what to treat and where to treat. Department trains students in Sharir Rachana by traditional and innovative methods using Ayurveda Samhita and modern literature. Regular active body donor programs cater by department to meet cadaveric requirement. We specially deal in aspect of Marma and Srotas Sharir along with other aspects of Sharir Rachana.

Faculty

Department performs different activities with renowned faculties.

AIM

As this is modern era- Department aims to train the students to be acquainted with classical knowledge of Sharir Rachana as described by our Acharyas along with modern literature of anatomy.

Facilities

  • Well equipped dissection hall and museum.
  • Normal Microscope for tissues studies are provided to students.
  • Normal and clinically relevant radiographs for student practice.
  • Charts especially related to Marma Sharir and Srotas Sharir to clear the concepts of Ayurvedic classics.
  • Lectures are conducted by audio-visual means.

Achievements

The most important achievement of the department was the successful conduction of CME (Teachers training program) in Rachana Sharir for teachers from all over India.
