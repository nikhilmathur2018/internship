Govt. Ashtang Ayurved Hospital, Lokmanya Nagar, Indore

The Hospital is located at College campus. Presently it's shifted in newly constructed building of approx 20,000 sq. feet area. It was inaugurated by Hon'ble Shri Jalam Singhji, Minster of AYUSH, Govt. of Madhya Pradesh. Presently OPD facilities are available for patient & IPD facility will be started shortly. It will be biggest Ayurved Hospital (capacity of 100 beds) in the city.

Highlights

  • All data is recorded & Maintained with the help of specialized computers & software.
  • Department wise 9 OPD sections.
  • Well-equipped Pathological Lab.
  • Free medicine distributed to all OPD patients.
  • There are Vast and fully equipped Panchakarma Theatres are available for male & female patients, separately. Panchakarma facility is also available in super specialty areas like – Pediatric panchakarma, Gynaecology Panchakarma. Well trained, experienced and well behaved therapists conduct the procedures under the direct supervision of Panchakarma Department.
  • Department of Shalakya Tantra perform Kriya kalpa procedures like tarpana, putpaka, seka ,aschyotana, anjana, pindi and vidalaka are being done in various eye related disorders. Kriya Kalpa is done in very common eye problems like myopia, hypermetropia, astigmatism, squint and for disorders like computer vision syndrome. Specific treatment for various retinal disorders like night blindness, glaucoma diabetic retinopathy etc is being provided in kriyakalpa with satisfactory results.
  • Under the Department of Agad Tantra & Vyavharayurved Deaddiction and Rehabilitation Centre is established.
  • Department of Swasthyavritt deals with patients suffering from life style disorders.
  • Fully equipped OPD of Dental diseases are also available.
  • Well supported by efficient staff & Ayurveda consultants.
  • For developing the immunity in children free Swran Prashan Sanskar at every Pushpa Nakshtra Muhrat in Department of Balrog (Paediatrics).
  • Special camps like AYUSH Roga Niyantran, School Health Check-ups, BMD and Diabetes-mellitus Camps etc. are conducted regularly by highly qualified Doctors.

Out Patient Services

Recently the OPD has been shifted to new building and hospital block. Well maintained, neat & clean and separate Daily OPD facilities are available in following Departments. Large numbers of patients are being facilitated at OPD levels along with minor Procedures.

  • Kayachikitsa (General Medicine)
  • Panchakarma
  • Shalakya tantra (Ophthalmology & ENT)
  • Dentistry
  • Strirog & Prasutitantra (Gynecology & Obstetrics)
  • Kaumarbhritya (Paediatrics)
  • Attayayik Chikitsa (Casualty OPD)
  • Swasthvritt (Community Medicine)
  • Agad Tantra

Specialty Clinics (Aayush Rog Niyantran OPD)

  • Lifestyle Clinic
  • Diabetes & Obesity Clinic (Madhumeha evam Medoroga Chikitsa)
  • Skin & Allergy Clinic (Tvak evam Anurjata chikitsa)
  • Infertility Clinic (Vandhyatva Chikitsa)
  • Arthritis, musculoskeletal & neuromuscular disorders Clinic (Vata Vyadhi Chikitsa)
  • Diabetic foot care & wound clinic (Prameha pidika & Vrana Chikitsa)
