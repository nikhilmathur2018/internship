Department of Swasthyavritt & Yoga

Introduction

Swasthyavritt & Yoga (Community Medicine & Yoga) is the subject that deals with prevention of diseases & healthy living of society.

School Health Camps

Department conducts school children's health examination camps from Ayurveda prospective in various schools. It also includes counselling of Preventive Medicine, Life style, Yoga & Home remedies.

Slum Area Services

Department also provides services with help of NGO's to children of slum areas neighbouring college campus. It covers counselling on home based food for Iron and Calcium deficiency.

Women Health Awareness

Department also conducted women health awareness program and kitchen based remedies in seasonal ailments with help of NGO's. (Repeated in original text)
