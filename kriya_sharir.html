Department of Kriya Sharir

Introduction

Department of physiology of Govt. Autonomous Ashtang Ayurved college, conducts teaching and practical classes for the physiology subject. In the laboratory of department necessary instruments‚Äô & facilities are available for various practical works related to physiology.

Practicals Conducted

  • Blood tests: hemoglobin test, blood group test, determination of number of different blood cells- RBC, WBC, platelets, PCV, ESR, Bleeding time, clotting time, preparation of blood smear, identification of different types of WBC, collection of blood sample etc.
  • Urine examination: physical examination of urine, chemical examination of urine ‚Äì test for presence of sugars, protein, phosphate, ketene bodies, bile salt-bile pigments in urine, specific gravity of urine etc.
  • Blood pressure measurement, measurement of pulse rate & respiratory rate, identification of heart sound & breath sounds, ECG, Body mass Index. Measurement spirometry, examination of sensory & motor nervous system.
  • Vision tests & hearing tests.
  • Ayurved practicals: assessment of prakriti, assessment of dosha-dhatu kshaya-vriddhi, assessment of Agni, assessment of koshtha, assessment for saar, nadi pariksha, mutra taila bindu pariksha etc.

Instruments Available

Microscope, hemoglobin meter, sphygmomanometer, thermometer, pyrometer, hydrometer, manometer, viscometer, glucose meter, stop watch, clinical hammer, tuning fork, peak flow meter, weighing machine, laryngoscope, centrifuge machine, Ichihara chart, Newton‚Äôs color wheel, refrigerator, stethoscope, electrocardiograph, ESR tube, RBC-WBC pipette.

Additional Facilities

Different charts and models for teaching purpose and necessary chemical reagents for various practicals are also available.

Paramedical Courses

Beside this department also conducts different theory & practical classes of physiology for paramedical courses conducted by the college.

Faculty

At present professor, lecturer, laboratory assistant & laboratory technician are working in the department.
