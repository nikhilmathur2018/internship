Principal's Message

Welcome to the Official Website of the Government Autonomous Ashtang Ayurved College, Indore, Madhya Pradesh.

Government Autonomous Ashtang Ayurved College, Indore, was established on 12-12-1972 by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Madhya Pradesh to promote Ayurveda in the society. The college is situated at Lokmanya Nagar, Kesar Bagh Road, Indore. Our Institute has received Pt.Uddhav Das Mehta Vaidhya Shastri Ayurved Seva Samman Puruskar-2016, from Ministry of AYUSH, Govt. of Madhya Pradesh, for the excellence work done in the field of Ayurved.

We are running various courses of department of AYUSH in our institution:

  • B.A.M.S. Five and a half year regular degree course with 75 seats.
  • Paramedical Courses:
    • D. Pharma (Ayurved), Two year course with 50 seats.
    • Certificate in Panchkarma Technician, One year course with 50 seats.

This Institute is running two hospitals in Indore, one at Lokmanya Nagar Institute campus and another at Rau, Indore. In which our Clinical and Non-Clinical staff is doing well to provide supreme health care services to the society. This institute is going to start soon a new hundred bed hospital in Lokmanya Nagar Institute Campus.

In upcoming years we are planning to start The Post-Graduation courses in various departments of Ayurveda.

We have very dedicated, skilled and highly qualified Faculty Members with a good number of skilled supporting staff in different categories, who take all challenges for achieving various goals.

We strongly believe in our motto of providing good facilities, which encourage quality education and academic excellence in every field. The Institute provides an extra edge to the students in this competitive world by developing overall personality. We believe that the knowledge is primary resource for the overall development of individual and society. We also believe in individual as well as general counseling of students and try to monitor the progress of each and every student. These efforts resulted into overall development of our students.

With these humble words, I once again welcome you to our Website. I shall be very happy to receive any suggestion/comment to make any addition/alteration in our Website.

Prof. Ajit Pal Singh Chauhan
Principal
