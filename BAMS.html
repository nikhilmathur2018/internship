B.A.M.S. (Ayurvedacharya)

B.A.M.S. ‚Äì Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery which is recognized by Central Council of Indian Medicine, New Delhi and affiliated with Madhya Pradesh Medical Science University, Jabalpur ( M.P.)

Seats

75 Students per year

Duration

This course includes 41/2 years and 1 Year compulsory rotating internship (Total 51/2 Yrs).

Admission Procedure

The students interested to take admission into B.A.M.S. course have to qualify the joint entrance test called NEET (National Eligibility Entrance Test) Conducted by agency authorized by Central Government, Which is usually conducted in the month ofJune-July every year. The merit listed students from NEET can only be allowed to take part in the online counselling which is conducted by AYUSH Department, Govt. of M.P. through M.P. online for admission in this Institution. Total 75 numbers of seats are available in this Institution for B.A.M.S. Course.

Eligibility

Higher Secondary (10+2) with Biology as Compulsory subject.

Reservation

Reservation as per rules of Govt. of Madhya Pradesh
