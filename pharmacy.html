Diploma In Pharmacy (Ayurveda)

Diploma in Pharmacy (Ayurveda) is a paramedical course to provide skilled and trained manpower to the society for Ayurvedic Medicine affiliated with Madhya Pradesh Medical Science University, Jabalpur and Madhya Pradesh Paramedical Council, Bhopal.

Seats

50 Students Per year

Duration

2 years

Admission Procedure

On the basis of Higher Secondary merit

ELIGIBILITY

Higher Secondary (10+2) with Biology as Compulsory subject.

RESERVATION

Reservation as per rules of Govt. of Madhya Pradesh

Fee Structure

Particular Amount (INR)
Course fee 47,910
Caution Money (Refundable) One time 5,000
Total 52,910

Scholarship

Post metric scholarship will be provided to the students of D. Pharma (Ayu) as per rules of Government of Madhya Pradesh.
