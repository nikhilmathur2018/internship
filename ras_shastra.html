Department of Ras Shastra & Bhaishajya Kalpana

Introduction

The department of Ras Shastra & Bhaishajya Kalpana in a whole may say the Indian Pharmaceutical science department. Ras Shastra deals with the drugs of metals & minerals origin, stones and gems, their varieties, characteristics, processing techniques, properties & their therapeutic doses where as Bhaishajya Kalpana deals in making of different kinds of other preparations used in Ayurvedic treatment. The department is also dealing in teaching, manufacturing & research activities.

Faculty

Ras Shastra & Bhaishajya Kalpana department is working well under the supervision of Reader& H.O.D. Dr. Vimal Arora. Department also have some other well qualified faculty as lecturer Dr. Amit Sinha and Dr. Preeti Hardenia.

Aim

The aim of the department is to manufacture ethically competitive and standard medicines fixed on scientific parameters as well as chemical, biological and toxicological parameters. Moreover to offer quality education, training, research competency development and consultancy to the satisfaction of all our stakeholders.

Utility

The subject trains the scholar in pharmaceutical process of different preparations used in our hospitals. Students are well motivated by our learned teaching and non-teaching staff up to that manner that our students should be skilful and focused after passing their courses for self- employment and to be a successful entrepreneur.

Facility

The department is having Facility of different kind of equipment, machinery, raw drugs used in Manufacturing of different Ayurvedic formulations along with some drug testing equipments. Different Ayurvedic formulations like Churna, Vati, Kwatha, Ointments, Medicated oil and Ghrita Preparations are frequently being prepared in the department and supplied to the associated hospital for patients. Practical oriented study is being conducted regularly. The department is highly engaged to guide and teaching paramedical diploma in pharmacy Ayurved Panchkarma technician students also.

Further Scope

Along with developing modern techniques of teaching, Standard Pharmacy can also be established Which should be well equipped with modern machineries In which the drug can be standardized from the very beginning like collection of raw drugs up to the processing and preparation of drugs including drug testing, its assessment by different parameters as well as quality control.

Current Project

Now a day's a research based Jatyadi Ghrita as a pilot project is being manufactured in the department and will be used on the chronic cancerous wounds in the Government cancer Hospital, Indore under the guidance of Department of Shalya Tantra of Govt. Ashtang Ayurved College, Indore (M.P.).
