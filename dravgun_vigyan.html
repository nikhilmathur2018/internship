Department of Dravyagun Vigyan

Introduction

One who has though knowledge of drug is a good physician and he/she can give pleasure to whole world. This is the basis of knowing the herbal medicine and research we deal in this. Dravyagun Vigyan is the basis of the entire subject as by this subject only student come to know the whole knowledge of the herbs. Department trains students in Dravyagun department by traditional and innovative methods using modern audio visual techniques, Ayurveda nighantu's and modern literature. Department has conduct educational tours for students to identification of herbal drugs available in various part of the country on regular basis.

Faculty

Dravyagun department is working well under the supervision of Dr. Hariom Parihar, Lecturer and Head of the Department with a Museum Keeper & an Attendant.

AIM

As this is modern era- Department aims to train the students to be acquainted with classical knowledge of Dravyagun Vigyan as described by our Acharyas along with modern literature of Pharmacology.

Facilities

  • A big herbal garden with 250 herbs.
  • A well-maintained herbal museum with more than 250 raw drugs.
  • Well-maintained department space for teachers, staff & students for quick reference.
  • Normal Microscope for T.S. studies are provided to students.
  • Lectures conducted by audio-visual means.
