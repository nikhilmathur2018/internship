Govt. Pt. Jawaharlal Nehru Memorial Ayurved Hospital, Rau

Govt. Pt. Jawaharlal Nehru Memorial Ayurved Hospital, Rau is our main hospital which is located at A.B. Road, Rau. There is only Govt. hospital in the town i.e. run by Govt. Ashtang Ayurved College, Indore. This is a state of art Hospital which follows all standards and guidelines which are prescribed by Central Council of Indian Medicine, New Delhi & AYUSH Department, Government of Madhya Pradesh.

Highlights

  • This is centrally located, easily accessible, well equipped and multiple storied building with 60 beds capacity.
  • All the OPD & IPD data is recorded & Maintained with the help of specialized computers & software to reduce consumption of paper and creating a Bio-Friendly Environment amongst Patients, Staff & Students.
  • Department wise 6 IPD & 8 OPD sections.
  • Various allopathic OPD are also available for patients.
  • Ayurvedic & Allopathic treatment administered simultaneously.
  • Well-equipped Pathological Lab.
  • Very moderate charges for other patients.
  • Physiotherapy and Yoga Therapy available.
  • Kaya-chikitsa and Panchakarma Departments are deals with various Granthokta and updated procedures for various Chronic Diseases.
  • It is great achievement for our hospital that, we performing almost 90 to 100 labor every month in Department of Stri roga & Prasutitantra (Obstetrics & Gynaecology).
  • Preventive care counseling given along with treatment.
  • Well supported by efficient staff & Ayurveda & Allopathic consultants.
  • Well-equipped separate O.T for general surgeries as well as ophthalmic surgeries.
  • Well-coordinated Vaccination units with the coordination of Civil Hospital, Indore.
  • 24x7 Ambulance facility is available.
